Blackjack Rules

Objective: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

  • Number cards are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (King, Queen, Jack) are worth 10.
  • Ace can be worth 1 or 11, whichever benefits the player more.
  • Each round starts with two cards.
  • You can choose to draw a new card ("Hit") or stay with your hand ("Stand").
  • If your sum goes above 21, you bust and lose the round.
  • Blackjack = An Ace + a 10-value card (Jack, Queen, King, or 10).
